Research Scientist

I am a researcher at the intersection of Scientific Machine Learning (SciML) and Scientific Foundation Models (SciFM). My research focuses on developing novel machine learning approaches for scientific applications, particularly in the domains of computational physics, 3D geometry, and large language models. I am passionate about bridging the gap between traditional scientific computing and modern AI techniques.

Research Interests

  • Scientific Machine Learning (SciML) with focus on PDEs and dynamical systems

  • Deep Learning for Scientific Computing

Research Overview

Scientific Foundation Models (SciFM)

Developing foundational AI models for scientific applications, focusing on understanding and generating scientific content, solving complex mathematical problems, and assisting in scientific discovery.

Scientific Machine Learning (SciML)

Combining traditional scientific computing methods with modern machine learning approaches, including neural networks for PDEs and physics-informed machine learning models.

3D Geometry & Computer Vision

Working on deep learning approaches for 3D shape analysis, geometric deep learning, and point cloud processing and analysis.
